PROGRAMMING CLASSES

In our after-school Programming Classes, you'll dive into the world of programming through fun, creative lessons designed to spark your curiosity and develop your skills. With topics like Scratch, Python, and computational thinking, you'll learn how to build your own games, apps, and animations!

CREATIVE WRITTING

Welcome to the Creative Writing Club, where your imagination takes the lead! Each term, we write a story together. One person starts with an exciting opening, then another member is randomly chosen to continue the story the following week — adding twists, new characters, and surprises! It’s a fun, collaborative way to explore storytelling.
