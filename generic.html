PROGRAMMING/CREATIVE WRITTING

Do you like technology and wonder how games, apps, or websites are made?

Then join our programming classes! You'll learn how to build your own projects, develop logical thinking, and discover how fun it is to turn ideas into code — no experience needed. Each week we explore a new theme — from designing your first website to writing simple games or cracking mini coding challenges. No prior experience needed — just curiosity, creativity, and a bit of that CTRL + C / CTRL + V energy. Step into the world of programming and see what you’re capable of!

Love writing or want to explore your creativity through stories?

Join our Creative Writing Club, where imagination meets teamwork! In the first week, we start a story together — then one person is randomly chosen to take it home and continue the tale. The next week, someone else picks up where it left off, building the story piece by piece, twist by twist. Whether you love writing, reading, or just want to have fun creating something unique with others, this is your place. No pressure, just creativity — one chapter at a time!
